Who are you ?

Hi ! My name is Sidonie 'Rune' Eckert, I'm a young french transgender artist very involved in queer rights and neurodivergent struggles; I want to be part of the artist building up the representation we highly lack of in the current medias online and on TV. As an artist, I'm very color and geometry focused, and for me a good simple color palette is worth as much as a big piece using all of the mediums and colors in the world. I come from a very small and rural area of northern-east France, in which we're taught a lot about how 'real life' works and in which a lot of people my age have to takeover over their parents' farms without getting the chance to try the jobs that they originally dreames of, and I want way more than that. Finally, I'd say my biggest quality is that due to my accidental occasional over focusing and obssessing on some of my projects and crippling anxiety keeping me from missing deadlines, I'm a pretty good artist to put your trust in and share your projects with. I'd love to put colors in your thoughts.
