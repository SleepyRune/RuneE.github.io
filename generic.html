Professional Projects

-As a young highschooler, I have a lot of projects and dreams in mind; so here's a simple recap.-

While at first I did plan on becoming a big name in the animation industry in the hope of giving future generations killer cartoons like the ones I had the joy to watch non-stop, I soon realized my absolutely not scholar brain wouldn't be able to handle the wait and scholar curriculum needed until then. As for now I'd say my brain is still oscilating between keeping art as a side job, selling art commissions and opening a small stickers business, and trying something more, something big, like in game design or become the art director of a future hit manga/anime. Who knows?

As for now my projects are all personal, like small pieces of animation and mini webcomics. Although, one of my big big project is still to somehow get a formation at a company I love, as this would clearly be the realization of a childhood dream. Amongst my works in progress are sitting a lot of pride and history related ideas, a lot of short films about various topic like gender dysphoria, being neurodivergent in a pretty neurotypical society and the never ending bigger and bigger expectations we set on new generations that in turn are less and less psychologically stable.
