Colorfully Creepy

If someone were to ask me what sums up my artstyle and artistic tastes, I think those few pieces would explain it very well; Creepy, bloody but make it cute and colorful. However, my style is varies a lot depending on my mood obviously, so sometimes I'll opt for something cooler in tones, calmer and simpler like this simple piece;

While my style could be considered simple or too childish, it's mainly a big melting pot of the styles of my favorite artists and insipirations; Ghibli movies, Anakama's Wakfu and Dofus, LavenderTowne from the Youtube channel with the same name, Zukich, Kasey Golden and of course Brandon J.G.
